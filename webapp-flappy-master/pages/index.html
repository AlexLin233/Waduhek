Welcome to Flappy Bird.

Three bonuses that are worth to know:

  • 炸弹会让你丢失3分
  • The TNT will drop you 3 marks.
  • 黄金无其他效果仅额外加1分
  • Gold only increases your score by 1.
  • 末影珍珠会让落地速度微微增加且加1分
  • Ender Pearl slightly increases the gravity and you also get 1 point.
  • 绿宝石会让落地速度稍微变慢一点并加1分
  • Emerald reduces the gravity by a small margin and you get 1 extra point.
